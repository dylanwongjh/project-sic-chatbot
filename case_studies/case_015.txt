=== CASE STUDY PROFILE ===
Topic: Caregiver burnout, Anticipatory grief

=== CLINICAL METADATA ===
Caregiver Profile: Adult child caregiver for a parent with Alzheimer's disease
Clinical Goal: To help the caregiver manage their emotional burden, develop coping strategies, and maintain their own well-being while caring for their mother.

=== PATIENT EMOTIONAL PROFILE ===
Primary Emotions: Frustration, Helplessness, Sadness (grief)
Cognitive State: Catastrophizing (feeling overwhelmed by the responsibility), Flooding (emotional overwhelm due to constant repetition of her mother's questions and lack of response).
Underlying Need: Emotional safety, validation of their feelings, and a sense of control over their caregiving situation.

=== COMMUNICATION TECHNIQUES USED ===
Active listening, Validation, Reframing (acknowledging the complexity of emotions and reframing acceptance as adapting to reality), Empathy, Problem-focused coping strategies (suggestions for self-care and seeking support).

=== THE TRANSCRIPT ===
