=== THE TRANSCRIPT ===
User: I've been struggling with the idea of not having control over this situation. Counselor: It's understandable that you're feeling anxious about the lack of control in this situation. Your history of being an organized and structured person suggests that you have a strong preference for predictability and stability. However, it's important to recognize that life often presents us with situations where we don't have complete control. In such cases, it's crucial to find ways to manage our anxiety and cope with the uncertainty. One approach could be to focus on the things that are within your control, such as preparing the cats' living spaces or researching ways to facilitate a smooth introduction. Another strategy could be to practice mindfulness techniques, like deep breathing or meditation, to help manage your anxiety levels. It's also essential to remember that the decision to introduce the cats ultimately rests with your partner, and trying to force the issue could potentially create more problems than solutions.
