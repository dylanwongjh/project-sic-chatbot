The practice of gratitude involves allowing yourself to fully engage in moments where you feel appreciation, even if they're fleeting. The third component of positive reappraisal is reframing, which is the ability to view situations from a different perspective. By focusing on mindfulness and gratitude, you'll be better equipped to catch your initial reactions and find alternative ways of viewing challenges. It's important to remember that the practice of positive reappraisal is a skill that takes time and effort to develop. It's not about denying the reality of your pain or the challenges you face, but rather about finding ways to approach them with a more positive and hopeful mindset.
