=== THE TRANSCRIPT ===
User: I've been haunted by guilt ever since my mother passed away last year. I can't seem to shake off the feeling that I didn't do enough for her, and I've been spending countless hours researching various treatments and protocols, hoping to find something that could have saved her.

In our next session, we'll explore the concept of grief and its various stages, as well as discuss coping mechanisms and healthy ways to express your feelings.
