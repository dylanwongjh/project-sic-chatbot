Topic: Caregiver burnout due to partner's anxiety and dependence

User: My wife's anxiety around eating and drinking without me has been escalating for months. Ultimately, the goal would be to help your wife develop the skills and confidence she needs to manage her anxiety independently, while also ensuring that both of you have the support and resources you need to maintain a healthy and balanced relationship. This might involve a combination of individual and couples therapy, as well as other interventions such as mindfulness practices, relaxation techniques, and lifestyle modifications.
