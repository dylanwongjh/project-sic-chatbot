=== THE TRANSCRIPT ===
User: I've been having nightmares about my father, who abandoned me when I was just a child. I feel like I can't move on from this trauma. I've tried talking to my friends and family, but they don't seem to understand the depth of my pain.
Counselor: Your experiences of abandonment and the resulting nightmares are understandably distressing. It's essential to acknowledge the profound impact of childhood trauma on your emotional well-being. I recommend that we explore the use of Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapy, which can help you process these memories in a safe and controlled environment. EMDR is an evidence-based approach that can help you develop resilience and improve your overall emotional health.
